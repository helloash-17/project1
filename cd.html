Data Science Engineering:


Data Science Engineering refers to the process of designing, building, and deploying data-driven solutions and systems, often with a focus on extracting valuable insights and knowledge from data.
Data science engineering combines principles and practices from various fields, including statistics, machine learning, computer science, and domain-specific expertise, to create data-driven applications and models.


Overview of Data Science Engineering:

  1. Problem Definition
  2. Data Collection
  3. Data Exploration and Visualization
  4. Feature Engineering
  5. Model Development
  6. Model Evaluation
  7. Deployment
  8. Collaboration and Communication
  9. Scaling and Optimization
