Computer Science Engineering:


Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is a course that combines computer engineering and computer science.
It's one of the most popular courses for engineering students.


CSE covers a wide range of topics, including:

  1. Computer networks
  2. Database management systems
  3. Analysis and design of algorithms
  4. Microprocessors
  5. Mobile communications
  6. Programming languages
  7. Digital electronics
  8. System software
  9. Performance evaluation
