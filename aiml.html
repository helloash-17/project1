Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Engineering:


AIML engineering are specialized fields within the broader domain of computer science and data science.
These fields focus on designing, developing, and deploying AI and ML solutions to solve real-world problems and automate tasks.
AI and ML engineers play a critical role in creating intelligent systems that can learn from data, make predictions,
and improve their performance over time.


Overview of AI and ML engineering:

  • Artificial Intelligence Engineering
    1. Problem Identification
    2. Algorithm Selection
    3. Model Training
    4. Evaluation and Testing
  • Machine Learning Engineering
    1. Data Preparation
    2. Model Selection
    3. Model Evaluation
    4. Continuous Improvement
